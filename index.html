Atlas

Designed and built with the intention of being a test vehicle. Atlas shares similar construction qualities as many high-powered rockets. While only supporting a rather small motor tube, the long term goal is to test solid engine propellents with this rocket.

  • Diameter: 3.1"
  • Weight: 1.14 lbs
  • Avionics Bay: 8"
  • Engine Mount: 29mm

Meet "Noggin"

The brains behind this whole opperation. Noggin' boasts a nRF52840 64MHz processor, 1MB Flash, and a plethora of sensors(see below) While only weighing 5grams and consuming 3.3v of power.

  • LSM9DS1 IMU (3D accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer)
  • LPS22HB Barometric Pressure Sensor
  • HTS221 (Temperature and Humidity) Digital Sensor
  • NINA-B306 (Bluetooth) Sensor
